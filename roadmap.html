Plan Your STEM Journey

This roadmap helps you track your progress and prepare for college or career goals in STEM.

  • Join a STEM club or coding workshop
  • Attend a local STEM fair or event
  • Apply to one internship or summer program
  • Start a small passion project or Raspberry Pi build
  • Submit to a science competition or hackathon
  • Find one free online STEM course to complete
  • Reach out to a mentor or professor
  • Apply for at least one STEM scholarship
  • Build a portfolio or GitHub project page
  • Share STEM Atlas with a friend!
