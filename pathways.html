Popular STEM Majors & Their Pathways

Civil Engineering

  • Take: Physics, Calculus, CAD, AP Enviro
  • Tools: AutoCAD, surveying tools, GIS
  • Do: Road or traffic safety project
  • Try: Future City, MATHCOUNTS, JETS

Computer Science

  • Take: AP CSP, AP CSA, Discrete Math
  • Tools: Python, GitHub, Scratch
  • Do: Make a game, website, or app
  • Try: Congressional App Challenge, HPE CodeWars

Biotech & Medical Research

  • Take: Bio, Chem, Anatomy, AP Bio
  • Tools: Pipettes, bioinformatics, labs
  • Do: Volunteer at hospital, do gene research
  • Try: Simons Program, Stanford SIMR, Neuroscience Prize

Environmental Science

  • Take: AP Enviro, AP Chem, Statistics
  • Tools: GIS, data logging sensors, Excel
  • Do: Water pollution testing, city green plan
  • Try: Stockholm Junior Water Prize, Envirothon
