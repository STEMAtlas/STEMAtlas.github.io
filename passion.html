Why Passion Projects Matter

Passion projects let you create something that reflects your interests, solves real problems, and helps others. They’re also one of the most meaningful things you can show on college applications.

Ideas to Get Started

  • Build a Raspberry Pi device to monitor air quality
  • Design a prototype to reduce speeding in your neighborhood
  • Start a local STEM workshop for middle schoolers
  • Document infrastructure issues in your city and propose fixes
  • Map out wheelchair-accessible entrances in public buildings

Group Projects

Want to collaborate? Group passion projects are a great way to build teamwork skills. Consider starting one with classmates or reaching out through your school’s STEM club.
